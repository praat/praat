Intro 5.1. Viewing formant contours

To see the formant contours of a sound as functions of time, select a Sound or LongSound object and choose View & Edit. A SoundEditor window will appear on your screen. The analysis part of this window will contain formant contours, drawn as red speckles. If you do not see the formant contours, choose Show formant from the Formant menu.
