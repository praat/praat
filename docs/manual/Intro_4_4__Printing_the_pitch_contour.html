Intro 4.4. Printing the pitch contour

To print a pitch contour, or to put it in an EPS file or on the clipboard for inclusion in your word processor, you first have to draw it into the Picture window. You do this by choosing Draw visible pitch contour... from the Pitch menu in the Sound or TextGrid window. From the File menu in the Picture window, you can then print it, save it to an EPS file, or copy it to the clipboard (to do Paste in your word processor, for instance).
