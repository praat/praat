Intro 6.1. Viewing an intensity contour

To see the intensity contour of a sound as a function of time, select a Sound or LongSound object and choose View & Edit. A SoundEditor window will appear on your screen. The analysis part of this window will contain an intensity contour, drawn as a yellow or green line (choose Show intensity from the Intensity menu if it is not visible). This also works in the TextGridEditor.
