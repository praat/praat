Intro 3.4. Printing the spectrogram

To print a spectrogram, or to put it in an EPS file or on the clipboard for inclusion in your word processor, you first have to paint it into the Picture window. You do this by choosing Paint visible spectrogram... from the Spectrogram menu in the Sound or TextGrid window. From the File menu in the Picture window, you can then print it, save it to an EPS file, or copy it to the clipboard (to do Paste in your word processor, for instance).
