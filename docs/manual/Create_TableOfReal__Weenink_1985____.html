Create TableOfReal (Weenink 1985)...

A command to create a TableOfReal filled with the first three formant frequency values from the 12 Dutch monophthongal vowels as spoken in isolation by either 10 men or 10 women or 10 children.

The three columns will contain the formant frequencies in Hz. Each row will be labelled with its corresponding vowel symbol.

More details about these data and how they were measured can be found in the paper of Weenink (1985).
